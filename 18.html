Final thought

You may have noticed that the experience remains different from each platforms for most of the Kotlin/Multiplatform libraries. But we, at Kodein Koders, are working on it.

Indeed, Kodein-DI is just one of the libraries from the Kodein Framework that we are developing to empower Kotlin/Multiplatform projects. And we want to improve our libraries to make them work seamlessly from every platforms targeted by Kotlin!
